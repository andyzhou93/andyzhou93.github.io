Lineup Setting Success

How were we at actually managing our teams? Did we make good adds, drops, and trades (lol)? How about picking the best starters? How many points did we leave on our benches?

Scoring Scenarios

Let's play out some scenarios where we could have been better (or worse) about setting lineups. I considered four scenarios, in order of increasing points scored (or at least I'd expect that...):

Draft Best Ball: Best ball scoring using only players from the original draft roster. No adds, drops, or trades reflected -- this is fully hands-off fantasy football.

Actual: The actual scores from the season based on our transactions and lineup decisions.

Actual Best Ball: Best ball scoring using each week's actual roster (including adds/drops). How could those bench points have helped?.

2-Week Pool: Best ball scoring using a combined pool of players from the current and previous week's rosters. Did you drop any guys that went off?
