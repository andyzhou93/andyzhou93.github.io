Player Performance vs Draft Position

How good were our draft picks? Did you run into bad luck with injuries? Were players just ass? I tried to calculate value for each player using Points Above Median (PAM), and then looked at individual players' PAM scores versus what was expected of players drafted in the same range. Jonathan Taylor was our league's biggest value. Biggests busts were mostly QBs that went down to injury (there you go Jackie), with Kaleb Johnson being the worst player to underperform due to being ass (lmao)

Points Above Median (PAM) measures how much a player outperformed or underperformed relative to the median player at their position: PAM = Total Points - Median Points for Position

Expected PAM is calculated by fitting a power curve to all 150 drafted players: Expected PAM = a * pick^b + c. Earlier picks should have higher expected PAM than later picks.

Value Delta = PAM - Expected PAM. Positive values are "steals" (exceeded expectations); negative values are "busts" (underperformed).
